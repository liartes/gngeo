-------------------------------------------
--------- gngeo for Trimui Model S --------
-------------------------------------------
0. Requirements
-------------------------------------------
Upgraded firmware to 0.105
MinUI as launcher
-------------------------------------------
1. Install
-------------------------------------------
Unzip the release ZIP

You must copy your NEOGEO bios (neogeo.zip) in
Emus/NEOGEO.pak/bios

Copy the content of the release ZIP + bios
on your Trimui SD card.

Emus/NEOGEO.pak contains the emulator
executable and the launch.sh script.

Drop your games (.zip only ) in Roms/NEOGEO

/!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\
  do not rename zip files 
  or games won't load anymore !
/!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\ /!\

Tested with some NEOGEO roms that shipped with 
the Trimui/Powkiddy, compatibility may vary...

You need to add at least one game to make the
NEOGEO emulator shows up in MinUI.

Emulator snapshots are stored in the folder :
Roms/NEOGEO/.gngeo
-------------------------------------------
2. Update
-------------------------------------------
Copy the content of the release ZIP on
your Trimui SD card, Depending of your system,
Say yes to "replace all" or "Merge folders"
-------------------------------------------
3. Uninstall
-------------------------------------------
Remove folders :
Emus/NEOGEO.pak
Roms/NEOGEO
